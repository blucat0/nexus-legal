Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-13

This Privacy Policy explains the basic way NexusHQ and NexusPanel handle information.

NexusPanel may store account information, tenant information, integration settings, cached device/customer/user data, audit logs, and system status information needed to provide the service.

Integration secrets and keys should be stored securely and should not be displayed back to users after saving.

NexusPanel is designed around tenant separation. Customer or tenant data should only be available to users authorized for that tenant.

Do not upload or enter secrets, private customer information, or sensitive data into public pages, screenshots, documentation, or support requests unless it is required and you have permission.
