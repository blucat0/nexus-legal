Acceptable Use Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

1. Use the service responsibly

NexusPanel is built for legitimate IT management, support, monitoring, and workflow use. You may only use it with accounts, customers, devices, networks, tenants, and integrations that you are authorized to access.

2. Do not use NexusPanel for abuse

You may not use NexusPanel to:

  • Access systems without permission.
  • Steal, expose, sell, or misuse data.
  • Harass, threaten, stalk, or harm others.
  • Send spam, phishing, malware, or malicious content.
  • Bypass access controls, authentication, rate limits, or tenant separation.
  • Scan, attack, overload, disrupt, or attempt to break the service.
  • Run unauthorized commands on devices.
  • Use agents on devices you do not have permission to manage.
  • Upload or publish secrets, passwords, tokens, keys, or private customer data.

3. No unauthorized monitoring or control

NexusPanel should not be used for hidden surveillance, unauthorized tracking, or device control without permission. If you install an agent or connect a customer system, you must have authorization to do so.

4. No risky public sharing

Do not publish private screenshots, customer names, internal system details, API keys, agent keys, credentials, tokens, database information, logs with secrets, or other sensitive data in public places.

5. Respect third party services

If NexusPanel connects to a third party platform, you must follow that platform’s terms, API rules, permission requirements, and rate limits.

6. Enforcement

If we believe this policy is being violated, we may suspend, restrict, disable, or remove access. We may also remove integrations, rotate or revoke keys, block traffic, or take steps needed to protect the service and other users.
