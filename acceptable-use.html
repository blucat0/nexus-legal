Acceptable Use Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-13

Use NexusHQ and NexusPanel only for systems you own, manage, support, or have permission to access.

Do not use NexusPanel for unauthorized access, abuse, harassment, data theft, credential theft, malware, spam, or attempts to disrupt systems.

Do not intentionally overload, scan, attack, reverse engineer, or abuse the service.

Do not publish real customer data, API keys, agent keys, passwords, tokens, or private screenshots in public documentation or support material.
