General legal questions

For questions about these legal documents, privacy, data handling, security reporting, or policy updates, contact NexusHQ through the official contact method listed on the main NexusHQ website.

Security reports

Please report security issues privately. Do not post public screenshots, exploit steps, tenant data, customer data, secrets, tokens, or private system information.

What to include

If you are reporting a security or privacy issue, include:

  • A clear summary of the issue.
  • The page, feature, or integration involved.
  • Steps to reproduce, if safe to share.
  • Whether any data may have been exposed.
  • Your contact information for follow up.

Do not include passwords, API keys, agent keys, client secrets, or private customer data unless we specifically ask for a safe way to receive them.
